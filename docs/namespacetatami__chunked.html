enum class tatami_chunked::SubsettedOracleSelection : char

Type of subset selection. Used to determine the subsets to extract in SubsettedOracleSlabCache::SubsettedOracleSlabSubset.

  • FULL: all rows/columns.
  • BLOCK: a contiguous block of rows/columns.
  • INDEX: an indexed subset of rows/columns.
